Our Top Features

Easy task management

Task addition is as simple as writing your tasks on a page. Once the tasks get defined you will be reminded to finish your tasks through whatsapp messages. In case you choose an accountability partner, they will help you plan your tasks, and review your tasks at the end of the day.

Conversational task manager

Our task manager is conversational in nature which means you can talk to our task manager to help improve your productivity.

We are currently using GPT-3 for the conversations and trying to make our task manager intelligent enough to help you plan and execute your daily tasks.

Trained accountability partners

You can choose to work with an accountability partner if you are struggling to plan or execute. Conditions like ADHD, substance use, anxiety and even stress can reduce productivity and increase procrastination.

Our accountability partners are trained to work with someone who is struggling to create right plans and execute the most important tasks of the day. They will help you get better at finishing your most important tasks and ultimately achieving your life goals.
